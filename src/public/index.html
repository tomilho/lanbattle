LAN Tank Battle

A couch game to play with up-to 3 friends.

Quick Introduction

To play you'll need all players to use their phone as controllers and a display device, such as laptop or tv, as the shared display. It is recommend to use a large screen so all players can be comfortable to play.

Creating the lobby

Simple click on "create a new party" button on the display device. You'll be redirected to a new page with a QR code. Simply scan the QR code with your phone and you'll join.
