LAN Tank Battle

A couch game to play with up-to 3 friends.

Quick Introduction

To play you'll need all players to use their phone as controllers and a display device, such as laptop or tv, to display the game. It is recommend to use a large screen so all players can be comfortable to play.

Creating the lobby

Simple press on "create a new lobby" on the display device. Soon you'll see a QR code
